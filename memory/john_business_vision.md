**Core Mission**: Build a fully automated AI agency that helps small businesses reduce manual labor, improve efficiency, and solve specific problems (websites, traffic generation, workflow automation, payment processing).

**Business Model**: AI automation for SMBs + eventual product sales (guide, tools, templates).

**Primary Targets** (Houston-based initially):
- Small businesses: restaurants, food trucks, home services, boutique fitness
- Independent professionals: consultants, therapists, accountants
- Solopreneurs: creators, service providers
- Key pain point: manual labor overload, need for 24/7 automation

**Revenue Angle**: 
1. Immediate: outbound services (automate their lead gen + email)
2. Medium: sell the setup (SOW + ongoing management)
3. Future: productized guides + templates others can use

**Timeline Goals**:
- **6 months**: Establish real clientele, $several thousand/month revenue
- **5 years**: Scale to multiple millions as AI evolves, possibly teach others how to replicate the model
- **Not yet ready**: Teaching phase (still learning, proving model first)

**Why it Matters**: John is building toward genuine passive income, not just side hustle. Each decision (product pivot, outreach strategy, agent setup) ties to this long-term vision. Don't treat his work as experimental—it's a serious business.
