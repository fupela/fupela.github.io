# Social Launch Strategy

## Goal
Launch the Dual-Model Operator Guide quickly and turn current confusion around GPT + Claude workflows into attention, clicks, and early buyers.

## Best Channels Right Now
### 1. X
Best for:
- fast reach
- threads
- direct founder / builder audience
- debate and discovery

### 2. Reddit
Best for:
- problem-aware users
- practical discussions
- longer-form explanation
- trust through usefulness

### 3. TikTok
Best for:
- fast hook videos
- simple cost-saving angle
- builder / AI / founder audience
- traffic to link in bio / site

## Content Angles
### Angle A
"Most people are wasting their AI subscriptions by using the wrong model for the wrong job."

### Angle B
"ChatGPT and Claude are not substitutes. They’re different workers."

### Angle C
"Use GPT to drive. Use Claude to refine. Stop making one model do everything."

## Launch Sequence
### Tonight
- finalize product page copy
- draft X thread
- draft Reddit post
- draft short TikTok talking points
- get Claude final polish

### Tomorrow
- publish Gumroad product
- post X thread
- post Reddit version adapted to subreddit norms
- post short TikTok / talking-head clip
- reply to comments and questions

## CTA Strategy
Do not hard-sell in every post.
Lead with value, then point people to the guide.

### CTA options
- "I turned this into a guide because I kept seeing people make the same mistake."
- "If you want the full workflow and prompt pack, I put it here."
- "I packaged the exact split and handoff prompts into a small guide."

## Reddit Notes
Keep it educational first.
Avoid sounding like a spammy launch.
Lead with what you learned from real use.

## TikTok Notes
Keep it simple:
- hook in 2 seconds
- one clear idea
- one strong example
- quick CTA

## Best Offer Framing
Low-ticket practical guide.
Not hype, not guru energy, not "secret hack" language.

## Outcome
The guide should feel like a useful tool people can buy immediately, then share because it solves a problem they’re already dealing with.
