FAQ

Questions answered.

What if the AI can't handle a weird situation?
It texts you the conversation in real-time. You can jump in anytime. Most calls are 100% handled by the AI — but you always have the wheel. Complex situations trigger a live transfer to your phone automatically.
How long until I'm live?
48 hours from our kickoff call. We build fast because we know you're losing calls right now. The kickoff takes 15 minutes — we map your services, scheduling rules, and dispatch logic.
Can my customers tell it's AI?
Most think it's a real dispatcher. The AI discloses it's an AI assistant upfront (required by law), but handles conversations naturally with your business's personality and service knowledge. It knows your pricing, service areas, and how to handle common scenarios.
What if I already have a receptionist?
Your receptionist gets promoted to customer success. The AI handles after-hours, overflow, and weekends — the calls they're already missing. No job losses. Better coverage.
Is this actual AI or prerecorded IVR?
Real conversational AI. It understands context, handles interruptions, and responds naturally. Not press-1-for-sales. If the customer changes their mind mid-call, the AI adjusts, re-qualifies, and updates the job summary accordingly.
What phone systems do you integrate with?
We work with any existing phone setup via call forwarding — Google Voice, RingCentral, your cell, or a dedicated business line. We can also provision a local Houston number at no extra cost. No hardware changes needed.
