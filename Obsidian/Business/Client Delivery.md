# Client Delivery

## Delivery Flow
1. Onboard client
2. Clarify offer and expected outcome
3. Gather assets and access
4. Build automation or outreach workflow
5. Launch and monitor
6. Report results
7. Recommend upsells or next steps

## Delivery Checklist
- [ ] Client intake complete
- [ ] Access collected
- [ ] Offer confirmed
- [ ] Workflow built
- [ ] QA completed
- [ ] Reporting setup complete
