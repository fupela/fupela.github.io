# Stripe Setup Guide — Step by Step Instructions

## Step 1: Create Your Stripe Payment Links
You need to create 5 payment links in Stripe. Each one is a secure checkout page for a product on your guides store.
---
### How to Create a Payment Link
1. Go to stripe.com and log in
1. Make sure Test Mode is ON (toggle in the top right — it should say "Test mode")
1. In the left sidebar, click Payment Links
1. Click the + New button

## Step 4: Go Live (When Ready)
When you're done testing and ready to accept real payments:
1. Toggle Test Mode OFF in the Stripe dashboard
1. Stripe will ask you to verify your identity and add a bank account
1. Recreate the payment links in Live Mode (same process, real money)
1. Send me the new live links and I'll update the site
