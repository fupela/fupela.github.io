# Section 3: TikTok Shop Strategy
## 3-Phase Roadmap
### Phase 1: Affiliate (Weeks 1–4)
1. Sign up for TikTok Shop affiliate program
1. Browse the affiliate marketplace and add 10–15 high-commission products (15%+) to your showcase
1. Post 1–2 product review videos daily using proven formats
1. Track which products and video styles get the most engagement
1. Goal: First commission check and 1,000+ followers
### Phase 2: Scale (Weeks 5–8)
1. Double down on winning products and video formats
1. Increase posting to 2–3 videos per day
1. Start building relationships with brands for higher commission rates
1. Cross-post content to Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts
1. Goal: Consistent $500+/month in affiliate commissions
### Phase 3: Dropshipping (Weeks 9+)
1. Use affiliate data to identify your best-selling product categories
1. Source those products directly from suppliers (CJ Dropshipping, Spocket, Zendrop)
1. Set up your own TikTok Shop store
1. Launch a Shopify store for long-term brand building
1. Goal: Full e-commerce business with multiple revenue streams
## Video Script Templates
Template 1: “TikTok Made Me Buy It” Review
- Hook (0–2s): “Okay TikTok made me buy this and I’m SHOOK”
- Demo (2–15s): Show the product in action, highlight the wow factor
- Verdict (15–25s): Give your honest take, mention the price, emphasize value
- CTA (25–30s): “Link in my TikTok Shop — trust me you need this”
Template 2: Before/After Demo
- Hook (0–2s): “Watch this transformation...”
- Before (2–8s): Show the problem clearly
- After (8–20s): Show the dramatic result using the product
- CTA (20–25s): “Get yours from my shop below”
## Hook Formulas That Work
- “Stop scrolling if you [problem]...”
- “I found the [product type] that actually works”
- “POV: You finally found [solution]”
- “This $[price] product replaced my $[expensive price] one”
- “3 things I wish I knew before buying [product]”
- “Run don’t walk — this is going viral for a reason”
