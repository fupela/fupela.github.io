## Script 20 — Overthinking
You are not thinking. You are punishing yourself with your imagination. A woman told me, Guruji, I cannot stop overthinking. My mind never shuts off. I said, your mind is not the problem. Your trust is the problem. You overthink because you do not trust yourself to handle what comes next. So you rehearse. You run every possible conversation in your head. You prepare for arguments that will never happen. You mourn relationships that have not ended. You solve problems that do not exist yet. My friend, you have fought a thousand battles in your head and not a single one of them was real. You know what overthinking really is? It is your mind trying to control the future because the past hurt you. Something surprised you once. Something blindsided you. And your brain said never again. So now it works twenty-four hours a day running simulations, trying to predict the next disaster. But here is the truth your brain will not accept. You cannot think your way to peace. You cannot analyze your way to happiness. You cannot prepare for every outcome. Life does not follow your script. It never has. The only cure for overthinking is action. Do the thing. Send the message. Make the decision. Even a wrong decision is better than no decision. Because a wrong decision teaches you something. Overthinking teaches you nothing except how to suffer in advance.

## Script 21 — Walking Away
The hardest walk you will ever take is the one where you walk away from someone who is standing right in front of you asking you to stay. A man said to me, Guruji, she keeps coming back. Every time I try to leave, she says the right things and I stay. I said, my friend, that is not love. That is a leash. And it is wrapped so gently around your neck that you mistake it for a necklace. Let me ask you something. When she says the right things, does she do the right things? Or does she just perform long enough for you to unpack your bags? Because words without change are just manipulation with better lighting. People will tell you what you need to hear the moment they feel you leaving. Not because they love you. Because they need you. And needing someone is not the same as loving someone. Love says I want you to be happy even if it is not with me. Need says I do not care if you are happy as long as you do not leave. Learn the difference. It will save your life. Walking away from someone you love is not weakness. It is the most violent act of self respect you will ever commit. And yes it will hurt. It will hurt like nothing has ever hurt before. But staying in the wrong place will hurt longer. Pain from leaving is sharp but short. Pain from staying is dull but forever. Choose the sharp one. It heals.

## Script 22 — Loneliness
There is a difference between being alone and being lonely. And most of you do not know which one you are. A young man said to me, Guruji, I am surrounded by people but I have never felt more alone. I said, that is because you are surrounded by the wrong people. Loneliness is not an empty room. Loneliness is a full room where nobody knows you. You have two hundred friends online and not one person you can call at three in the morning when your world is falling apart. You have a group chat with forty people and not one real conversation. You are performing connection instead of experiencing it. My friend, you do not need more people. You need more depth. One person who truly sees you is worth more than a thousand who follow you. But here is the part nobody tells you. Before you can find that person, you must first be able to sit with yourself. Alone. In silence. Without reaching for your phone. Without turning on a show. Without texting someone you do not even like just to fill the quiet. Because if you cannot be alone with yourself, you are not looking for connection. You are looking for distraction. And there is a very big difference. Loneliness is not solved by adding people. It is solved by removing the mask. Show someone who you really are. Not the version that gets likes. The version that gets lonely. That is where connection lives. In the real. In the ugly. In the honest.

## Script 23 — Patience / Success
Everybody wants the fruit but nobody wants to water the tree. A boy told me, Guruji, I have been working so hard and nothing is happening. I said, how long have you been working? He said, three months. I laughed. I said, three months? My friend, the bamboo tree grows underground for five years before it breaks the surface. Five years of watering. Five years of nothing to show. Five years where everyone around you says you are wasting your time. And then in six weeks it grows ninety feet tall. People look at it and say, wow, that happened fast. No it did not. It took five years and six weeks. You just only saw the six weeks. That is your life right now. You are underground. You are building roots that nobody can see. Not your friends. Not your family. Not even you. But the roots are growing. Every video you posted that got twelve views. That is a root. Every rejection email. That is a root. Every night you stayed up working while your friends went out. That is a root. And one day, one day you will break through the surface and everyone will say you are lucky. You are not lucky. You are patient. And patience is the most disrespected skill in a world that worships speed. The internet shows you twenty year olds with Lamborghinis and tells you that you are behind. You are not behind. You are building. And what you are building takes time. Respect the process or the process will replace you with someone who does.
