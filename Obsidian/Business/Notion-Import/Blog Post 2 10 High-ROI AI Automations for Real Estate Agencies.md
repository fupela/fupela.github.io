# Blog Post 2: 10 High-ROI AI Automations for Real Estate Agencies

## Real Estate is a Relationship Business — But the Admin is Killing You
The average real estate agent spends 30% of their day on administrative tasks: scheduling viewings, updating CRMs, and sending follow-up emails. This is time that should be spent closing deals.
In 2026, the most successful agencies are using AI to handle the "grunt work." Here are 10 automations you can deploy today:
1. Instant Lead Response: 24/7 AI chatbots that qualify leads and book viewings.
1. Automated Listing Updates: Send personalized property matches to your database based on their search history.
1. Viewing Follow-Up: Automated SMS surveys sent 30 minutes after a viewing to capture feedback.
1. Contract Drafting: Use AI to pull data from your CRM and populate standard contracts.
1. Social Media Content: Automatically turn new listings into Instagram Reels and LinkedIn posts.
1. Review Requests: Automated emails sent to happy clients 48 hours after closing.
1. Invoicing & Commission Tracking: Sync your sales data with QuickBooks or Xero.
1. Client Onboarding: Automated welcome packs and "Next Steps" guides for buyers and sellers.
1. Database Reactivation: AI-driven email campaigns to re-engage "cold" leads from last year.
1. Weekly Performance Dashboards: Get a summary of your team's metrics delivered to your inbox every Monday.
