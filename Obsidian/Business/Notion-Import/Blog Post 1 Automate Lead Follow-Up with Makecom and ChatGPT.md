# Blog Post 1: Automate Lead Follow-Up with Make.com and ChatGPT

## The 60-Second Rule: Why Your Leads Are Going Cold
In today's fast-paced digital economy, speed is everything. Research shows that responding to a lead within 5 minutes increases the likelihood of conversion by 900%. Yet, most business owners are still manually replying to emails hours or even days later.
By the time you hit "send," your prospect has already moved on to your competitor.
### The Solution: The "Jedai Flow" 60-Second Lead Responder
Using Make.com and ChatGPT, you can build a system that:
1. Captures the lead from your website (Typeform, Facebook Ads, etc.).
1. Analyzes the lead's intent using ChatGPT.
1. Responds with a personalized email or SMS that addresses their specific pain points.
1. Notifies you instantly on Slack or WhatsApp.
### How to Build It (The 5-Step Blueprint)
1. Trigger: Set up a "Watch Responses" module in Make.com for your form builder.
1. AI Analysis: Send the form data to the OpenAI module with a prompt like: "Analyze this lead's inquiry and draft a helpful, professional response in under 100 words."
1. Personalization: Use the lead's name and mention their specific industry.
1. Delivery: Send the email via Gmail or Outlook and an SMS via Twilio.
1. CRM Sync: Automatically add the lead to your CRM (Pipedrive, HubSpot, etc.) with the AI's summary.
