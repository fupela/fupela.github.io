# Business Project — TikTok Shop & Freelancing Strategy

---
## Dropshipping Platform Comparison
### The Smart Play (2026)
Use TikTok Shop as your traffic and product validation engine (test what sells through viral content), then funnel winning products to a Shopify store for long-term brand building.
---
## TikTok Shop — Fastest Route: Affiliate
### Step 1 — Get Approved
Go to TikTok Shop Seller Center or the TikTok app → Creator tools → TikTok Shop. You typically need 1,000+ followers.
### Step 2 — Pick a Niche
Best-selling categories: beauty/skincare, health supplements, kitchen gadgets, phone accessories, cleaning products.
### Step 3 — Add Products to Your Showcase
Browse the affiliate marketplace, filter by commission rate (aim for 15%+), add products with good reviews and proven sales.
### Step 4 — Make Content That Sells
Winning formats: "TikTok made me buy it" reviews, before/after demos, unboxings, "things you didn’t know you needed." Keep videos under 60 seconds, hook in first 2 seconds.
### Step 5 — Scale What Works
Once a video gets traction, make variations. Double down on winning products.
---
## Freelancing — Making Money Today
### Quick-Start Platforms
- Fiverr / Upwork — Gigs in writing, video editing, social media management, data entry, VA work, graphic design. Can get orders within days.
- TaskRabbit / Thumbtack — Local work (moving, furniture assembly, cleaning). Same-day pay.
- Rev / GoTranscript — Transcription work, start almost immediately.
- Respondent / UserTesting — $50–200 per session for testing websites or research studies.
### Recommended Approach
Do both simultaneously: Use freelancing for immediate income while building TikTok Shop presence on the side.
