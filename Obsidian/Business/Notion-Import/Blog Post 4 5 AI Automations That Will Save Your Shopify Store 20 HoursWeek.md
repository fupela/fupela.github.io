# Blog Post 4: 5 AI Automations That Will Save Your Shopify Store 20+ Hours/Week

## The "Scaling Wall": Why Most Shopify Stores Plateau
You’ve hit product-market fit. Your ads are working. Your revenue is growing. But so is your overhead.
Suddenly, you're spending all day answering "Where is my order?" emails, manually updating inventory sheets, and chasing down suppliers. You're no longer the CEO; you're a full-time customer support rep.
### The Solution: The "Jedai Flow" E-commerce Stack
Using Make.com and AI, you can build a self-running store that handles the "grunt work" while you focus on brand and strategy.
1. The "WISMO" (Where Is My Order) Agent: Automatically pull tracking info from Shopify and send a personalized SMS or Email update. No human intervention needed.
1. AI Customer Support Bot: Connect ChatGPT to your help desk (Zendesk, Gorgias). It can handle 70% of common queries instantly using your brand's voice and policy.
1. Automated Inventory Alerts: Build a workflow that monitors stock levels and automatically drafts a Purchase Order (PO) in your email when levels hit a specific threshold.
1. Personalized Post-Purchase Nurture: Use AI to analyze a customer's purchase and send a tailored "How to use your [Product Name]" guide 2 days after delivery.
1. Review & UGC Request Pipeline: Automatically send a review request 7 days after the tracking shows "Delivered." If they leave a 5-star review, send an automated "Thank You" discount code for their next purchase.
