## Week 1: Plant Seeds (Days 1-7)

### Day 1 (TODAY) — Post + Outreach
1. **Post #1** (Launch Announcement) on LinkedIn — copy from social-posts.md
2. **Post #5** (Free Audit Offer) on Facebook — copy from social-posts.md
3. **Send 5 cold emails** — Open Gmail drafts, personalize [First Name] and details, send to:
   - 2 local real estate agents (find on Google Maps)
   - 2 local coaches/consultants (find on Instagram or LinkedIn)
   - 1 e-commerce store owner (find on Shopify store directories)
4. **Activate your Make.com auto-responder scenario**

### Day 2 — Communities
1. Join 3 Facebook groups: "Small Business Owners", "Real Estate Agents [Your City]", "Online Coaches"
2. DON'T sell. Answer questions, be helpful. When someone asks about automating something, say "I actually do this — DM me"
3. Post **#2** (Pain Point Post) on LinkedIn

### Day 3 — More Outreach
1. Send 5 more personalized cold emails (different people)
2. Comment on 10 LinkedIn posts from business owners in your target industries
3. Post **#3** (Value Bomb / 3 Free Automations) on LinkedIn + Facebook

### Day 4 — Local Networking
1. Search "[your city] business networking events" on Eventbrite/Meetup
2. Sign up for at least 1 event this week
3. Send 5 more cold emails

### Day 5 — Follow Up
1. Follow up on ALL unanswered cold emails from Day 1 (1 sentence: "Just bumping this up — any interest?")
2. Post **#4** (Case Study) on LinkedIn
3. DM 5 people who engaged with your posts

### Day 6-7 — Weekend Push
1. Send 5 more cold emails
2. Engage in Facebook groups (answer 10 questions)
3. Post the Free Audit Offer in 2 Facebook groups (check group rules first)

**Week 1 Target: 5 meetings booked**

## Week 2: Double Down (Days 8-14)

### Day 8 — Refine & Scale
1. Check what emails got replies — send more of THAT type
2. Send 10 cold emails (you should be faster now)
3. Post a new LinkedIn post: "I've been doing free automation audits this week. Here's what I'm seeing..." (share 2-3 anonymized insights)

### Day 9 — Partner Outreach
1. Find 3 web designers / marketing agencies in your area
2. Email them: "I build the automation backend for businesses. If any of your clients need AI/automation help, I'd love to partner. I'll send referral fees your way too."
3. Send 5 more cold emails to prospects

### Day 10 — Video Content
1. Record a 60-second video: "3 tasks you should automate today" — post on LinkedIn + Instagram
2. Send 5 more cold emails
3. Follow up on Week 1 unanswered emails

### Day 11 — Double Down on What Works
1. Whatever channel generated the most replies — do 2x of that today
2. Send 10 more cold emails
3. DM every person who liked/commented on your posts but hasn't booked

### Day 12 — The Big Push
1. Post the Free Audit offer EVERYWHERE: LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, any group you're in
2. Email everyone who opened but didn't reply (use a different subject line)
3. Ask anyone you've talked to: "Know anyone else who might benefit from this?"

### Day 13-14 — Close the Gap
1. Direct DM blitz: message 20 people on LinkedIn who are in your target industries
2. Follow up on EVERYTHING
3. If you're short on meetings, offer "free automation teardown" — record a Loom of yourself showing how you'd automate their specific business (5 min video). Send it cold. This converts like crazy.

**Week 2 Target: 10 more meetings (15 total)**

## The Math
- 70 cold emails x 5% reply rate = 3-4 meetings
- 10 social posts x 2 DMs each = 5-6 meetings
- Facebook groups + referrals = 3-4 meetings
- Follow-ups convert 20% of opens = 2-3 meetings
- **Total: ~15 meetings**

## Rules
1. NEVER sell in your first message. Ask about THEIR problem first.
2. ALWAYS follow up. 80% of deals happen after the 2nd-5th touch.
3. Offer the audit for FREE. The money comes from the build, not the call.
4. Track everything in your Notion pipeline.
5. Consistency beats perfection. 5 bad emails > 0 perfect emails.
