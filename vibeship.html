Ship in three steps

No config files. No setup docs. Just run it.

Step 01

Scan

VibeShip audits your project for common vibe-coding gaps — missing error handling, exposed secrets, broken imports, unoptimized assets.

Step 02

Fix

Auto-applies safe fixes. Flags anything that needs a human decision. You stay in control — VibeShip just does the tedious part.

Step 03

Ship

Deploy to your platform of choice with a clean, production-ready build. No last-minute surprises. No "it worked on my machine."
