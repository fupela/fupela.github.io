Why Most Missed Calls Happen

  • On the job: The tech is under a sink, in an attic, or behind a panel. Can't hear the phone.
  • After hours: The customer calls at 7PM. Everyone's home. Nobody answers.
  • During peak: Monday morning, first freeze, rainstorm — phones ring nonstop. One person can't handle it.
  • Voicemail death: 60-70% of callers hang up at voicemail. They don't leave messages.
