## Post 3: Value Bomb

**Best on:** LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter/X

3 automations any business can set up this week (for free):

1. Auto-sort your inbox.
Most email platforms let you create rules and filters. Set up automatic labels or folders for client emails, invoices, and newsletters. Takes 20 minutes. Saves you from scanning 100+ emails every morning.

2. New client welcome sequence.
Use a free tool like Make or Zapier (free tiers are generous) to trigger a welcome email + a Slack/Teams notification to your team the moment someone fills out your intake form. No more "oh I didn't see that come in."

3. Meeting notes to action items.
Record your next client call (with permission), run it through an AI transcription tool, and have it automatically drop a summary + action items into your project management tool. Your team knows what to do before the call even ends.

None of these require code. None of them cost money to start. All of them will give you back hours every week.

## Post 4: Social Proof / Case Study

**Best on:** LinkedIn, Facebook

Here's what happens when you automate client onboarding:

We worked with a consulting firm that was onboarding about 12 new clients a month. Their process looked like this:

- Manually send a welcome email (sometimes forgot)
- Wait for the client to fill out an intake form
- Chase them 2-3 times if they didn't
- Manually create their folder structure in Google Drive
- Add them to the project management board
- Notify the assigned team member via Slack

Total time per client: about 45 minutes of scattered work across 3 days.

After building out their automation:

- Client signs the proposal and everything kicks off automatically
- Welcome email sends instantly with the intake form link
- Automatic reminders go out at day 2 and day 5 if the form isn't completed
- Google Drive folders generate themselves from a template
- Project board card creates with all the client details pre-filled
- The assigned consultant gets a Slack message with everything they need

Total time per client: zero. It just happens.

That's 9 hours a month they got back. But more importantly, nothing falls through the cracks anymore. Every client gets the same professional experience from day one.

This isn't futuristic stuff. This is available right now, and it's simpler to build than most people think.
