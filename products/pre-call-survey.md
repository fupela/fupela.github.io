## FOR INTERNAL USE — Survey Logic

If they select:
- **"8-12 calls" or "13+ calls" missed/week** → HOT lead. They feel the pain daily. Call them immediately.
- **"4-7 calls" missed/week** → WARM lead. Book a meeting.
- **"1-3 calls" or "no idea"** → COOL. Send the numbers, follow up in a week.
- **"$800+" average job** → HIGH VALUE. Prioritize.
- **"Voicemail — most never leave one"** → Pain point confirmed. Lead with that in the call.
- **"I call them back"** → Opportunity: "How long does it take you to call back? 80% of people call the next guy within 5 minutes."
