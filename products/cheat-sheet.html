5 Automations You Can Set Up in 5 Minutes — No Coding Required
1

Lead Auto-Response

Tools: Google Forms + Make.com + Gmail
  1. Create a Google Form for inquiries (name, email, need)
  2. In Make.com, connect "Watch Form Responses" to a Gmail "Send Email" module
  3. Write a template: "Hi {{name}}, thanks for reaching out! We'll reply within 24 hours."
2

Appointment Reminder

Tools: Google Calendar + Make.com + Twilio (or Gmail)
  1. In Make.com, add "Watch Calendar Events" set to trigger 24hr before
  2. Connect to Twilio SMS or Gmail module
  3. Template: "Reminder: You have an appointment tomorrow at {{time}}. Reply to reschedule."
3

Review Request Email

Tools: Google Sheets + Make.com + Gmail
  1. Add completed clients to a Google Sheet (name, email, service date)
  2. In Make.com, watch for new rows and schedule a 3-day delay
  3. Send: "Hi {{name}}, how was your experience? Leave us a review: [link]"

5

ROI Calculator

Tools: A calculator (or your brain)
  1. Estimate hours saved per week by automating a task (e.g., 3 hrs)
  2. Multiply by your hourly rate or employee cost (e.g., 3 x $50 = $150/week)
  3. Compare monthly savings vs. tool cost to get your ROI
Monthly ROI = ((Hours Saved x Hourly Rate x 4) - Tool Cost) / Tool Cost x 100%
