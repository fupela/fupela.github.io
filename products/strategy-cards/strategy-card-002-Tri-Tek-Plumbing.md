# Strategy Card — Tri-Tek Plumbing LLC
_Prepared by Pela | 2026-04-18 | HOT — Batch-6_

---

## The Business
- **Owner:** Tommy Alderete Jr., Master Plumber & Founder
- **Company:** Tri-Tek Plumbing LLC
- **Website:** tri-tekplumbing.com
- **Email:** tritekplumbingco@yahoo.com
- **Category:** Plumber, family-owned, 18+ years Houston

---

## The Pain Point
Eighteen years in Houston means Tommy has a real reputation to protect — and a phone that customers actually trust. But family-operated means the phones still rely on people. During a busy stretch (summer humidity + slab leaks + AC-adjacent plumbing calls), the whole crew is on job sites. Every call that hits voicemail is a customer calling someone else.

The "18 years of experience" angle that wins Tommy jobs also means customers *expect* him to answer. When he doesn't, they move fast.

**Estimated miss rate:** 25–35% during peak hours/busy season. Potentially higher after hours.

---

## What We'd Build for Tommy
An AI phone agent that:
1. **Answers as "Tri-Tek Plumbing"** — professional, branded, knowledgeable
2. **Knows the service menu** — residential/commercial, water heaters, re-pipes, drain cleaning, emergency service
3. **Qualifies and prioritizes** — distinguishes emergency (active leak/burst) from scheduled work
4. **Books scheduled jobs** directly to calendar — Tommy gets a text with all details
5. **Warm transfers emergencies** to Tommy's cell immediately if it's a genuine emergency

---

## The Math to Use on the Call
"You've been around 18 years — your reputation brings the calls. What kills you is when those calls hit voicemail. If you miss 4 jobs a week at $350 each during peak season, that's $5,600 a month. We're $199. It's not even close."

---

## Call Angles
- **Opener:** "You've built a real business over 18 years. The problem isn't leads — it's what happens to calls when your guys are all on jobs."
- **Angle:** Experienced customers call Tri-Tek *because* they trust Tommy. If he doesn't answer, they feel let down. That's a loyalty risk, not just a missed sale.
- **Demo close:** Call the demo. Show how it handles "I have a water heater that's making noise" — let Tommy see it actually knows what to do.

---

## Setup Notes
- 18 years = likely has established processes → frame AI as an upgrade to their existing system, not a replacement
- Yahoo email signals low-tech comfort level → keep setup conversation simple: "works with your phone number, nothing to install"
- Family operation means Tommy probably does the admin too — AI reduces his back-office burden
- Good candidate for the ROI angle: missed-call cost vs. $199/mo is obvious
